#### Supply chain risks
Hardware manufacturers
- Conduct rigorous risk assessments on the supply chain
- Purchasing from other sellers
Software providers
- Integrity checks
- Antivirus scans
- Known vendors
Managed service providers
A good msp should be able to
- Evaluate data security measures
- ensure confidentiality and integrity 
- assess cyber security protocols
- response to the security breach
#### Supply chain attacks
targeting a weaker link in the supply chain to gain access to primary target.
chip washing in counterfeited hardware.
Ensure
- Vendor due diligence
- Regular monitoring audits
- Education and collaboration
- Incorporating contractual safeguards

#### Vendor selection and monitoring
Due diligence
- financial stability
- operational history
- Client testimonials
- on-the-ground practices
Conflict of interest
- bias the judgement
Vendor questionnaires
- comprehensive documents that potential vendors fill out to offer insights into their operations, capabilities and compliance.
Rules of engagement :
- Communication protocols
- Data sharing policies
- negotiation boundaries
Always monitor the vendor.
Feedback loops are a two way communication channels to improve each other by collaborating.

#### Contracts and agreements
Foundation stones
Basic contract
- formally establishes relationship
- a formal tool
Service level agreement(SLA)
- standard of service a client can expect
Memorandum of Agreement(MOA)
- outlines responsibilities of parties involved
Memorandum of Understanding(MOU)
- declaration of mutual intent
Master service agreement(MSA)
- covers general terms of engagement across multiple transactions
Statement of work(SOW)
- Specifies details for specific project
Non disclosure agreement(NDA)
- Commitment to privacy
Business partnership agreement(BPA)
- two entities decide to pool their resources for mutual benefit.
